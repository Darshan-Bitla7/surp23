Deformation mechanism
Professor: Soumyajit Mukherjee
UID: ES01

ES01

  • Description: How poroelastic materials deforms in ideal condition will be studied in the geological contexts. The supervisor will set the boundary conditions. Students, preferably from engineering backgrounds and with considerable knowledge on poroelasticity mathematics, will solve the problems.
  • Number of students: 4
  • Year of study: Students entering 2nd year, Students entering 3rd year, Students entering 4th/5th year
  • CPI eligibility criteria: 7
  • Prerequisites: The student must have studied photoelasticity course(s) from the mathematical perspective
  • Duration: Entire summer vacation of IIT Bombay in 2023
  • Learning outcome: Ideal deformation cases will be modeled/simulated. Publications expected in quality platforms.
  • Weekly time commitment:40 hours
  • General expectations: Self motivated, keen to solve mathematical problems. If required., software can be used such as COMSOL.
  • Assignment: I want to make a video where I will state the problem on black board. Please arrange a recording in CDEEP.
  • Instructions for assignment: This can be done after the student watches the video.
